French Onion Rice

Easy and savory rice dish any member of the family would love to eat.

    Ingredients

  • 1 Can of Campbell's French Onion Soup
  • 1 Can of Campbell's Beef Broth
  • Half stick of unsalted butter
  • Long grain white enriched rice

Instructions

  1. Pour cans of french onion soup and beef broth into pot;
  2. Use empty can to measure rice (one full can of rice);
  3. Cut half stick of butter onto rice; and
  4. Cook in rice cooker or on stove at medium heat.
